LaksNet

An end-to-end deep learning model for autonomous driving in Udacity simulator.

Future Technologies Conference (FTC) 2023 scheduled in Nov 2023 in San Francisco accepted my research paper on this and will be published soon in Springer series. Nominated for Emerging Research Award by FTC committee. Developed a new CNNmodel called ‘LaksNet’ with four convolutional layers and two fully connected layers. Achieved state of the art performance with ‘LaksNet’ model after conducting extensive experiments and it outperformed compared to NVIDIA and the other pre-trained models.
